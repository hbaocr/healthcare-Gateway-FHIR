Clinics

  • Pay Eth/Token to regist/update their info with smartcontract
  • Insert the FHIR records to patients if they have suitable permission
  • Pay Eth to service provider to update and store FHIR records
  • Request the permission from patients to access patients' records in certain time
  • Pay Eth/Token to patients to have the permission to access their FHIR records (not implement yet)

Patients

  • Pay Eth/Token to regist/update their info with smartcontract
  • Having full permission with their FHIR records
  • Forbid /Approve the Clinics' permission to access their FHIR records in certain time

FHIR Service Provider

  • Keeping the FHIR records in their server
  • Get Eth/token fee from submitting/updating participants' info
  • Base on smartcontract to verify if the requesters having the valid permission to access data

FHIR Ethereum SmartContract

  • Keep tracking the accesses tx from participants
  • Store the Id of FHIR records and their ownership
  • Manage on chain the permission to access the FHIR records
